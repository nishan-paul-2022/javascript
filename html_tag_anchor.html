LOKI : A PERFECT PORTRAYAL OF THE MULTIVERSE SAGA

God of mischief turns into God of stories, giving us the best character arc of the long 15 years of MCU. Neither a charismatic villain nor an anti-hero anymore, not even some mainstream hero who saves the world and lives happily ever after. Rather, he evolves as the savior of the whole multiverse, the watcher and the container of all possible stories that have ever been written or will be written. Finally found his glorious purpose, and yes, none can justify this title rather than him. The classic LOKI scene is the new definition of goosebumps. If it's not the best, surely the second-best iconic moment of the whole MCU. Thanks for everything, and kudos to Tom Hiddleston. This skinny, tall, long-haired handsome man is surely the biggest star in the current franchise. A 10/10 performance that left us no choice but to crush on him. LOKI is now more than a character, an emotion, isn't he?

So, what is LOKI up to this time? What's his glorious purpose?

At the very beginning of the MCU, multiple timelines, aka multiverse, do exist. In every timeline, for every person, there are some nexus events (which we have seen previously in Spider-Verse). So, what is a person's nexus event? In a nexus event, a new timeline is created for every possible choice a person can make. So, for every nexus event, an infinite number of timelines are created. In every new timeline, for every person, there are also nexus events. So, the creation of timelines goes on infinitely. This is fine unless some mad-level genius named Kang finds out about it and decides to travel across the multiverse. As we have seen previously in Doctor Strange, frequent multiversal travel causes time incursion, and time incursion causes timeline declination. So, an infinite number of Kang variants from an infinite number of timelines start a multiversal war against each other to save their respective timelines. Because any Kang from any timeline is a potential threat to cause time incursion to others'. So, a timeline can only survive declination if it is the only one that exists. One of the Kang variants (he-who-remains) from the sacred timeline made TVA in a timeless zone. The core of TVA is a temporal-loop that uses cosmic energy to convert the timeline into a physical entity called a branch. By using the temporal-loop, he-who-remains ensures to destroy every timeline except his own, that has ever been created or would be created. He-who-remains paves the way for every living entity in his timeline to ensure no new timeline can occur. There is a high-value cost of doing that: no free will. Anyone can be an outcast (or variant) because of his own choice. Every time someone does something in a nexus event that goes against his predestined decision decided by he-who-remains, he will be treated as a variant. When the existence of a variant crosses a certain threshold, a new timeline and a new branch will be created. This creation will go on and increase exponentially. It will cause an overflow in the temporal-loop. The temporal-loop will explode and destroy TVA. He-who-remains will lose his dominance on the timeline. He surely can create another temporal-loop. But multiple timelines mean multiple Kang. So in the meantime, again another multiversal war will occur. To prevent that from happening, a variant needs to be pruned. Pruning means sending the variant to the timeless zone where the variant can't cause timeline creation. TVA can locate any variant from any moment using a tempad, powered by the temporal-loop. TVA is in charge of pruning the variant for the sake of the integrity of the sacred timeline. But there may be a variant who will just refuse to get pruned and decide to break the chain. To do that, this variant will kill he-who-remains. So that no one will get pruned for making his own choice. Now people have free will. Free will leads to multiple timelines. Multiple timelines mean multiple Kang and new branches on temporal-loop. So temporal-loop will overflow, explode, and destroy TVA. Without temporal-loop, it will not be possible to stop Kang. Kang will find out about the multiverse and start to travel across multiverse. This will lead to time incursion and time incursion will lead to multiversal war. The loop goes on endlessly. It's like replacing one nightmare with another one repeatedly for an infinite number of times. Now LOKI is up to breaking the loop, saving multiverse, giving people free will. That's his glorious purpose.
